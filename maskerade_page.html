MASKERADE

A narrative-driven survival horror game developed in Unreal Engine 4 and nominated for the 'Best College Videogame' award by BIG. In this first-person game, you play as a young girl forced to work as a ballerina in a sinister cabaret, trying to escape the clutches of the Madame. Developed as a vertical slice for my Master's TFM in Game Programming, the project was created in collaboration with students from the Design and Art Master's programs.


My contributions:

  • Developing inventory system
  • Developing condition-based dialogue system
  • Puzzle implementation
  • Production management
