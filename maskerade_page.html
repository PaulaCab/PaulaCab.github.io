MASKERADE

A narrative-oriented horror game developed in Unreal Engine 4, nominated for the "Best College Videogame" award by BIG. The project serves as a vertical slice created for my Master's TFM in Game Programming, in collaboration with students from the Design and Art Master's programs.


In this project, I was responsible for developing several key systems, such as an inventory system, a condition-based dialogue system and custom puzzles.
