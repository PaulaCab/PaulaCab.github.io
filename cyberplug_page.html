CYBERPLUG

Cyberplug is a group project developed in Unity during my fourth year in the Game Design program. This fast-paced 2D platformer puts players in control of an electric being capable of moving at high speeds and using its abilities to traverse obstacles and overcome enemies. The game focuses on fluid movement and responsive controls, offering a gameplay experience that tests players' reflexes and precision.


My contributions:

  • Developing gameplay mechanics
  • Progression & saving implementation
  • User interface implementation
  • Environmental art props
