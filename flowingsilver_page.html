FLOWING SILVER

Developed in collaboration with students from Nord University in Norway, this project is a social awareness game that follows the journey of a young child navigating a war-torn country in a desperate quest to bring water home. Through its mechanics and challenges, the game sheds light on the harsh realities and struggles faced by civilians caught in conflict zones, offering players a experience that fosters empathy and understanding.


My contributions:

  • Enemy AI programming
  • Shader creation
  • Props 3D modeling
