About me

I’m Paula Cabezas, a game programmer specialized in Unreal Engine and creating immersive, high-performance gameplay systems. I thrive on solving complex technical challenges and creating seamless, responsive gameplay experiences.

My approach to coding prioritizes efficiency, clean code, and scalability while maintaining high-quality gameplay.


Passionate about both the technical and creative sides of game development, I’m always looking for new opportunities to collaborate on projects that challenge my skills and drive innovation.
