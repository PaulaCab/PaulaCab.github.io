DOMENATION

A free-to-play third person shooter, which its gamemode uses time as the main resource, since once the player runs out of it, he will die. Players must seek refuge in domes to halt their dwindling time or venture out to steal time from others.


My role in this project includes developing gameplay systems, crafting user interfaces, and addressing bug fixes and performance optimization, while also managing team dynamics to ensure smooth project progression.
