DOMENATION

A free-to-play multiplayer third person shooter, which its gamemode uses time as the main resource, since once the player runs out of it, he will die. Players must seek refuge in domes to halt their dwindling time or venture out to steal time from others.


In this project, I had the opportunity to lead the programming department, overseeing the development process and ensuring the implementation of efficient workflows. My role involved designing and developing gameplay systems, optimizing performance, and integrating networking features to enhance the online experience. Additionally, I collaborated closely with other departments to create a seamless and engaging player experience.


My contributions:

  • Developing gameplay systems
  • User interface implementation
  • Networking integration
  • Bug fixing & optimization
  • Team management
